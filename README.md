# 🛒 Amazon Product & Review Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Amazon product listings, pricing strategies, discounts, customer ratings, and reviews** to uncover insights about customer satisfaction, product popularity, and marketplace trends.

The objective is to transform raw Amazon product data into **actionable business intelligence** through:

- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Statistical Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Business Recommendations

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

This analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

### 📦 Product Performance

- Which products are most popular?
- Which products generate the highest customer trust?
- Which categories dominate the marketplace?

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction

- Which categories have the highest ratings?
- Which products receive the most reviews?
- What drives customer trust and engagement?

### 💰 Pricing & Discounts

- Do higher discounts improve ratings?
- Do expensive products receive better ratings?
- Which categories offer the highest discounts?

### 🚀 Marketplace Intelligence

- Which categories deserve more investment?
- Which products should Amazon promote?
- Which categories have untapped potential?

## 🔄 Project Workflow

This project follows a structured **12-Phase Analytics Framework**:

### 1️⃣ Business Understanding

Defined business objectives and analytical questions.

### 2️⃣ Data Understanding

Explored dataset structure and variables.

### 3️⃣ Data Quality Assessment

Checked missing values, duplicates, and data consistency.

### 4️⃣ Data Profiling

Analyzed products, categories, ratings, and discounts.

### 5️⃣ Feature Engineering

Created:

- Discount Amount
- Popularity Score
- Price Segment
- Rating Category

### 6️⃣ KPI Reporting

Built key business metrics and marketplace KPIs.

### 7️⃣ Distribution Analysis

Analyzed distributions of ratings, prices, discounts, and review counts.

### 8️⃣ Business Analysis & Visualization

Created business-focused visualizations.

### 9️⃣ Statistical Analysis

Performed descriptive statistics and correlation analysis.

### 🔟 Segmentation & Advanced Analytics

Used GroupBy, Pivot Tables, and Crosstab Analysis.

### 1️⃣1️⃣ Executive Insights

Generated business-focused insights.

### 1️⃣2️⃣ Recommendations

Provided strategic recommendations.
