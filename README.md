Amazon Product & Review Analytics
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon product listings, pricing strategies, discounts, customer ratings, and reviews to uncover insights about customer satisfaction, product popularity, and marketplace trends.

The objective is to transform raw Amazon product data into actionable business intelligence through data cleaning, feature engineering, statistical analysis, visualization, and strategic recommendations.

🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

Product Performance
Which products are most popular?
Which products generate the highest customer trust?
Which categories dominate the marketplace?
Customer Satisfaction
Which categories have the highest ratings?
Which products receive the most reviews?
What drives customer trust and engagement?
Pricing & Discounts
Do higher discounts improve ratings?
Do expensive products receive better ratings?
Which categories offer the highest discounts?
Marketplace Intelligence
Which categories deserve more investment?
Which products should Amazon promote?
Which categories have untapped potential?

🔍 Key Findings
Customer Satisfaction
Most products maintain strong ratings, indicating generally positive customer experiences.
Customer trust is concentrated among a limited number of products.
Product Popularity
A small group of products dominates customer engagement and review activity.
Highly reviewed products tend to maintain strong ratings.
Pricing & Discounts
High discounts do not necessarily lead to higher ratings.
Premium products continue to maintain competitive customer satisfaction.
Marketplace Trends
Certain categories dominate customer engagement and marketplace visibility.
Customer attention is not evenly distributed across products.
💡 Business Recommendations
Recommendation 1

Promote highly rated products with strong review counts through recommendation systems.

Recommendation 2

Increase visibility of top-performing categories to maximize engagement.

Recommendation 3

Focus on improving lower-rated products using customer feedback analysis.

Recommendation 4

Avoid excessive discounting where customer satisfaction remains unchanged.

Recommendation 5

Prioritize products with strong popularity metrics in marketing campaigns.
